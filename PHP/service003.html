EXTRA LEGROOM SEAT

*Travel in comfort with ample space to stretch out. You can now pre-book an extra legroom seat in Economy Class for a small additional fee. Seats with extra leg space are available for pre-booking subject to availability, eligibility criteria and terms and conditions. Pre-booking of extra legroom seats is available only for passengers holding Quickfly issued tickets and on SriLankan operated flights, and is not available on our partner/code share flights.
